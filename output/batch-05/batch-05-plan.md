## C051 | Back-Row Dragged Out → Grandmother's Birthmark Found

**Source:** Concept 01 #3 — The Back-Row Stranger  
**Opening style:** Silent witness  
**Setting:** Grace Chapel, Savannah, Georgia — Saturday 5pm, warm amber light

**4-beat arc:**
- **Setup:** Security drags quiet woman from final pew during wedding; bride's family demands removal.
- **Clue:** Bride sees crescent birthmark on woman's wrist matching grandmother's description of vanished aunt.
- **Turn:** Groom's mother turns white; groom recognizes woman's lullaby hum from rehearsal recording.
- **Payoff:** Woman is bride's biological aunt, hidden after family scandal; groom's family hid adoption records to control inheritance.

**Peak-tension:** Two security guards gripping woman's arms; bride in lace frozen mid-aisle; torn program on floor; woman's wrist birthmark visible.

**Character Lock:**
- Ava Thornton — 27, dark curls, ivory gown, trembling hands
- Miriam Cole — 59, gray twist, navy dress, crescent birthmark on inner wrist
- Setting — Savannah chapel, wooden pews, amber stained glass, Saturday 5pm
- Object clue — crescent birthmark on wrist (Act 3–4)
