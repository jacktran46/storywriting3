## C041 | Lot Sweeper Mocked → Dealership Founder Returns

**Source:** Concept 03 #4 — The Parking Lot Cleaner  
**Opening style:** Room goes wrong  
**Setting:** Prestige Auto Gallery, Scottsdale, Arizona — Saturday 10am, harsh desert sun through glass showroom

**4-beat arc:**
- **Setup:** Sales manager humiliates a gray-haired man in coveralls for touching a $400K sports car with a broom.
- **Clue:** The man's worn leather work gloves have a faded dealership logo from 1987; regional director's car pulls up outside.
- **Turn:** Director enters mid-berating and asks why the founder is on his knees scrubbing tire marks.
- **Payoff:** Founder built the 14-dealership group; manager is fired on the spot; founder reveals he sweeps lots monthly to see who respects working people.

**Peak-tension moment (photo):** Manager jabbing finger inches from sweeper's face while pointing at muddy boot print on chrome fender; sweeper holds broom low, jaw tight, not backing down; luxury buyers watching from velvet ropes.

**Character Lock:**
- Marcus Hale — 67, silver stubble, faded navy coveralls, cracked leather gloves, slight limp left knee
- Derek Voss — 38, slick black hair, charcoal suit, gold watch, aggressive posture
- Setting — Scottsdale luxury auto showroom, glass walls, desert sun, polished concrete
- Object clue — faded "Hale Motors" patch on glove cuff (pays off Act 3–4)
